// How it works

Three steps.
That's it.

No setup wizard. No credit card for a trial. Text "setup" and you're running.

STEP_01 📱

Text "setup"

Text the word "setup" to your team's number. We'll walk you through naming your team and adding members — all over SMS.

You: setup
Nudge: Welcome! What's your team name?
STEP_02 💬

Manage in plain English

Create tasks, assign them, mark things done, check status — all by texting naturally. No commands to memorize.

You: Add logo design for Jake, due Thursday
Nudge: Done — assigned to Jake, due Thu Mar 13.
STEP_03 🔗

Share the dashboard

Text "dashboard" to get a read-only link. Share it with stakeholders. It updates live — no login needed for them.

You: dashboard
Nudge: Your link (24h): usenudge.co/d/a8f2...
// Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for teams who'd rather ship than sit in standups.

🚫

Zero installs

Your whole team already has SMS. No app to download, no account to create, no password to forget. If you have a phone, you're set.

🧠

Plain English

Powered by Claude AI. Say "finish the homepage" or "what's Sarah working on?" — it understands you, not the other way around.

☀️

Morning digest

Every morning at 8am, each team member gets a personalized summary of their open tasks, overdue items, and what's due today.

📊

Live dashboard

A clean, read-only web view of everything your team is working on. Grouped by owner, filterable by status. Share it with anyone.
